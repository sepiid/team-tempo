About Us

ZIIIRO

The silicone-metal straps of watch models ZIIIRO Gravity, Orbit and Aurora are made to fit a wrist size of about 16.00 cm – 18.50 cm. They can be bent a little bit to match lower and larger sizes. If your wrist circumference is below 16 cm, the watch will have a loose fit and vice versa.

PAUL GROTESKY

The ZIIIRO Gravity, Aurora & Orbit are fully interchangeable with all watches from the bracelet series, also labelled by the interchangeable-logo. Simply pop out the clock body to set the time or mix and match it with other strap colors.

MINIMAL DESIGN

Water Resistant 3 ATM or 30 m. Suitable for everyday use. Splash/rain resistant. NOT suitable for showering, bathing, swimming, snorkelling, water related work and fishing. If you don't take your watch for a swim, you should be fine.

  • It all began on the Internet where Robert, based in Germany, posted his watch designs. They caught Derick’s attention, who was halfway across the globe in Hong Kong. In March 2001, the two of them started ORA.
    Robert: “I was absolutely thrilled at having the chance to take my designs into production. We spent the following nine months on research and development. One big hurdle was that neither of us had any product or watch design background."

  • In 2004, Francois Thiébaud was appointed head of ORA and initiated the creation of two gravity lines: the T-Collection, designed by a woman for women in 2005, a collection with many different editions that also later included men’s models, all graced with the “T” of the interchangable wrist band; This technology allowed the wearer to enjoy real instrumental information, such as that from a compass, altimeter, barometer, and more.

  • In 2006, Hei came onboard as the third member of Team ORA and was in charge of all technical aspects of production. With everything in place, it wasn’t long before ORA launched its first two models, GRAVITY and MERCURY, on their custom-built webstore.

  • In 2010 ORA partnered with Tissot, Overwhelmed by the positive response, Robert and Derick realized that with the ORA brand they created a perfect platform for subsequent ideas to become true. Regarding the way how we work on new projects together, not much has changed since the beginning.

  • The ORA Protégé Arts Initiative was founded in 2014 to encourage talented individuals through a unique design to make time for fun. Futuristic, minimalist, bold. Every design embodies the ORA vision of creating incredible timepieces with unique appearance and style. These space-age timekeepers remind us to zig while the world zags, and to take all the time we need to live and enjoy a life that’s awesome.
